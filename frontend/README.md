# Water Tariff Dashboard

The Water Tariff Dashboard is a digital tool developed in Task 4.3 of the InnWater project and integrated into the InnWater Governance Platform. It is designed to evaluate and simulate the socio-economic impacts of household water pricing policies, with a particular focus on Reunion Island as a pilot case InnWater D4.4 InnWater.

## Purpose and Functionality

The Water Tariff Dashboard uses a microsimulation model to assess the performance of domestic water tariffs across five key dimensions aligned with the European Water Framework Directive (E-WFD):

- **Affordability** – Are water services economically accessible to households?
- **Incentive Effect** – Do pricing strategies effectively promote water-saving behavior?
- **Equity** – How are the financial burdens and benefits distributed across income groups?
- **Cost Recovery** – Does the tariff system ensure sustainable funding of water services?
- **Economic Efficiency** – Are resources allocated optimally without distortion?
