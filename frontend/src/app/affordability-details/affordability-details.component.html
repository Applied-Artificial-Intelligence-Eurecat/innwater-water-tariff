Affordability Details

The Affordability Details module provides detailed statistics about the Payment Affordability Ratio (PAR) for different household groups. It compares the PAR under the Increasing Block Tariff (IBT) and the Two-Part Structurally Balanced Tariff (TBSE). The module also provides Gini index decomposition to analyze inequality in affordability.
